# Phase 135: Automatic Reprice, Renew, Decline, and Bind Orchestration - Context

## Goal

Execute bounded automatic reprice, renew, decline, and bind behavior over the pricing and capital substrate.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC only reaches insurer-grade automation once pricing outputs can drive controlled execution rather than remaining advisory.

## Scope

- automatic reprice and renewal flows
- bounded decline and bind execution
- rollback and human-interrupt points
- lifecycle evidence for automated decisions

## Out of Scope

- federated trust contracts
- wallet-network interop
- final maximal-endgame closure
