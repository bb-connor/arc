# Phase 133: Autonomous Pricing Artifacts and Authority Envelopes - Context

## Goal

Define bounded autonomous pricing artifacts and the authority envelopes under which pricing automation may act.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC cannot automate insurer-like behavior until pricing outputs and authority boundaries are explicit signed surfaces.

## Scope

- autonomous pricing artifacts
- authority envelopes
- model input provenance
- explainability and approval hooks

## Out of Scope

- capital-pool optimization
- automatic bind execution
- final qualification closure
