# Plan 109-02

Define ledger-event semantics over capital-book state.

## Scope

- debit, credit, lock, reserve, and release event semantics
- provenance linkage to facilities, bonds, and governed approvals
- counterparty and role attribution rules

## Success Criteria

- capital-book changes are event-driven and auditable
- every event ties back to a source of authority
- ambiguous counterparties or currencies fail closed
