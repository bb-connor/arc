# Phase 109: Capital Book and Source-of-Funds Ledger - Context

## Goal

Turn ARC's current facility, reserve, and bond posture into an explicit live
capital-book model with attributable sources of funds.

## Why This Phase Exists

The full endgame cannot claim live capital allocation until ARC has one
deterministic capital ledger that ties economic exposure to named funding
sources and role boundaries.

## Scope

- live capital-book artifacts and balances
- source-of-funds attribution across facilities, reserves, and bonds
- role-aware ledger events and provenance
- fail-closed mixed-currency and missing-counterparty handling

## Out of Scope

- escrow instruction execution
- governed-action allocation engine behavior
- automatic claims payment and recovery clearing
