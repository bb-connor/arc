# Phase 80: Governance, Dispute Semantics, and Marketplace Qualification - Context

## Goal

Close `v2.17` with explicit governance, dispute, and qualification semantics
for ARC's public certification marketplace surfaces.

## Why This Phase Exists

Marketplace-grade discovery is incomplete without public revocation,
supersession, dispute, and audit flows. This phase makes those governance
boundaries explicit and proves the public publish-discover-consume path end to
end.

## Scope

- dispute and governance state for public certifications
- public evidence updates, revocation, and supersession workflows
- qualification for publish, discover, resolve, and consume flows
- milestone audit and planning-state advancement into `v2.18`

## Out of Scope

- credit, capital, or bonded-autonomy implementation
- permissionless or auto-trusting marketplace semantics
- regulatory claims beyond ARC's evidence and workflow posture
