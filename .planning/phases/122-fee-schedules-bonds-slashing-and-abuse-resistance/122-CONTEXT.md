# Phase 122: Fee Schedules, Bonds, Slashing, and Abuse Resistance - Context

## Goal

Define marketplace fee schedules, publisher or dispute bonds, slashing, and
abuse-resistance economics over the open-market substrate.

## Why This Phase Exists

The endgame needs open-market economics and discipline, but those controls must
be explicit, signed, and bounded rather than emergent or informal.

## Scope

- fee-schedule artifacts and economic policy metadata
- publisher, listing, or dispute bonds
- slashing and abuse-resistance controls
- fail-closed behavior for stale authority or incompatible economic policy

## Out of Scope

- adversarial multi-operator qualification
- final partner proof and release-boundary closure
- hosted publication or external legal review processes
