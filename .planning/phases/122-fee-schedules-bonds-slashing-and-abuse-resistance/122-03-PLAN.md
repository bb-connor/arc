# Plan 122-03

Document marketplace economics and abuse-resistance boundaries.

## Scope

- operator and participant guidance for fees, bonds, and slashing
- rejection rules for stale authority or incompatible economic profiles
- public protocol language for open-market discipline

## Success Criteria

- marketplace economics are legible to operators and partners
- failure and appeal paths are documented clearly
- docs preserve explicit non-goals around ambient trust or automatic global penalties
