# Phase 88: Qualification, Operator Controls, and Sandbox Integrations - Context

## Goal

Close `v2.19` with qualification, simulation controls, and one sandboxed
integration path over bonded execution.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC should not claim bonded autonomy until the reserve, gating, and loss-
recovery paths are reproducible under qualification and visible to operators.

## Scope

- bonded-execution qualification and simulation
- operator controls and kill-switch semantics
- sandbox or external-capital integration proof
- milestone audit and planning-state advancement into `v2.20`

## Out of Scope

- provider registry and quote workflows
- claim adjudication flows
- hosted release publication
