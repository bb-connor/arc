# Phase 82: Credit Scorecards, Probation, and Anomaly Signals - Context

## Goal

Turn exposure history into one versioned credit-scorecard contract with
explicit probation, anomaly, and explanation semantics.

## Why This Phase Exists

The research does not stop at underwriting decisions. It explicitly points
toward agent credit and bounded capital allocation. ARC needs a stable,
explainable score posture over exposure truth before capital-facility or bond
work can be justified.

## Scope

- scorecard dimensions and weighting model
- probation, downgrade, and anomaly semantics
- reason-coded explanations over exposure truth
- fail-closed handling for sparse, stale, or contradictory evidence

## Out of Scope

- capital-facility issuance
- reserve-backed autonomy gating
- marketplace provider workflows
