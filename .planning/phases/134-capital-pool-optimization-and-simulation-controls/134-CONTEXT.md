# Phase 134: Capital-Pool Optimization and Simulation Controls - Context

## Goal

Optimize reserve and capital allocation across facilities, pools, and claims through explicit policy plus simulation controls.

## Why This Phase Exists

Pricing automation needs explicit capital posture and simulation controls before it can safely influence live allocations.

## Scope

- capital-pool optimization policy
- reserve strategy artifacts
- simulation and scenario comparison
- operator-visible allocation reasoning

## Out of Scope

- automatic bind orchestration
- final autonomous qualification
- federated trust networking
