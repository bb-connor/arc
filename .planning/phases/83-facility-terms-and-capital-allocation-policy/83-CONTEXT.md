# Phase 83: Facility Terms and Capital Allocation Policy - Context

## Goal

Bind credit posture into signed capital-facility terms and bounded allocation
policy that can later support bonded autonomy and provider review.

## Why This Phase Exists

Score without allocation is only descriptive. The research's next layer is
bounded capital. ARC therefore needs one explicit facility contract that ties
credit posture, assurance, and certification into capital ceilings and
prerequisites.

## Scope

- capital-facility term schema
- bounded allocation and ceiling policy
- score, assurance, and certification prerequisites
- facility supersession and denial semantics

## Out of Scope

- reserve lock state
- autonomy-tier execution gating
- provider quote or claim workflows
