# Phase 395 Plan 01

## Objective

Ensure the public A2A and ACP surfaces advertise only the lifecycle and
authority semantics they really provide.

## Work Items

1. Separate real A2A blocking and streaming/task semantics, or narrow the
   shipped interface/docs to the blocking semantics actually implemented.
2. Separate real ACP lifecycle capabilities from design-only futures, or
   narrow the shipped interface/docs to the blocking JSON-RPC surface actually
   implemented.
3. Harden compatibility-surface isolation so receipt-bearing authoritative
   execution remains the obvious default/public path.
4. Add deterministic collision handling for outward discovery and publication
   instead of silent dropping.
