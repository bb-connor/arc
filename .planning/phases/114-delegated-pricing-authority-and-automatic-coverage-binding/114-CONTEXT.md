# Phase 114: Delegated Pricing Authority and Automatic Coverage Binding - Context

## Goal

Add bounded delegated pricing authority and automatic coverage binding inside
one explicit provider or regulated-role envelope.

## Why This Phase Exists

The research endgame expects runtime underwriting to become live market
infrastructure. ARC needs a tightly bounded delegated pricing and bind model
before it can claim that step honestly.

## Scope

- delegated pricing authority artifacts
- automatic coverage-binding semantics under explicit limits
- linkage to underwriting, capital, and provider policy truth
- fail-closed handling for stale authority or out-of-envelope bind requests

## Out of Scope

- automatic claims payout rails
- recovery clearing or reinsurance settlement
- open registry and governance-network work
