# Phase 239 Context

The new activation package only counts if its claims and handoff rules are
explicit and product-owned. This phase defines the bounded assertions Mercury
is allowed to make, the approval refresh boundary, and the customer handoff
model for one selective-account activation motion.

Everything in this phase must stay inside Mercury's app surface and docs. It
must not drift into generic onboarding workflows, customer platforms, or ARC-
side commercial controls.
