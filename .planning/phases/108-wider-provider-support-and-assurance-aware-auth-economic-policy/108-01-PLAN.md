# Plan 108-01

Extend the common appraisal and federation substrate to additional providers.

## Scope

- additional verifier or provider family adapters over shared contracts
- mapping and compatibility rules for new provider assertions
- fail-closed handling for unsupported family-specific semantics

## Success Criteria

- ARC supports wider provider coverage on one portable substrate
- new families use the shared contract rather than bespoke truth models
- unsupported semantics remain explicit and bounded
