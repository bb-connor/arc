# Phase 108: Wider Provider Support and Assurance-Aware Auth/Economic Policy - Context

## Goal

Widen provider coverage over the shared appraisal and federation substrate and
thread that posture into downstream authorization and economic policy.

## Why This Phase Exists

The federation milestone is only credible if the shared contracts support more
than the original provider set and the resulting assurance can influence other
ARC control-plane decisions explicitly.

## Scope

- wider provider or verifier family support over the common appraisal model
- assurance-aware authorization and economic policy hooks
- fail-closed treatment for unsupported provider semantics
- milestone qualification and boundary updates

## Out of Scope

- live capital-book execution
- escrow or reserve instructions
- open registry and governance-network work
