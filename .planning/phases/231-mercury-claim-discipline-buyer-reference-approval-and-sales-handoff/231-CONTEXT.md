# Phase 231 Context

With the reference-distribution package defined, Mercury now needs the
operating model that controls what can be said, who approves it, and how the
bundle gets handed to a landed-account motion. Those controls must stay
Mercury-owned and bounded.

Non-goals:
- do not create a generic marketing-claims system
- do not widen into CRM or pipeline tooling
- do not broaden the approved claim beyond the bounded evidence stack
