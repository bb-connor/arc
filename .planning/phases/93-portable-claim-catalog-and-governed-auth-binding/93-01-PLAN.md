# Plan 93-01

Define the portable claim catalog and provenance-preserving identity layers.

## Scope

- standardize the portable claim set ARC is willing to support across profile families
- separate ARC-native provenance identity, portable issuer identity, and portable subject binding identity
- keep `did:arc` as ARC's internal provenance anchor without forcing it to be the only portable subject form

## Success Criteria

- ARC has one explicit portable claim catalog instead of one ad hoc projection
- subject and issuer binding rules are machine-readable and auditable
- unsupported rebinding patterns fail closed
