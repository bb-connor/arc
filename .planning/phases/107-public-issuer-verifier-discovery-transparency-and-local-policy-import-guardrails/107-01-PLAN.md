# Plan 107-01

Define and publish public issuer and verifier discovery artifacts.

## Scope

- public metadata endpoints or listings for issuers and verifiers
- provenance and signature requirements for discovery objects
- compatibility rules with existing ARC public metadata surfaces

## Success Criteria

- issuers and verifiers have one explicit public discovery surface
- discovery artifacts are signed, versioned, and auditable
- unsupported or partial discovery objects fail closed
