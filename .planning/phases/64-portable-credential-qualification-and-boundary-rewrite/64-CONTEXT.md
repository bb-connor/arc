# Phase 64: Portable Credential Qualification and Boundary Rewrite - Context

## Goal

Close `v2.13` with qualification evidence and updated boundary language for
the new standards-native portable credential lane.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC should not claim the portable credential gap is closed until the new path
is externally legible, regression-covered, and clearly documented alongside the
existing ARC-native lane.

## Scope

- raw-HTTP and verifier-facing qualification
- protocol, portability, and release doc updates
- milestone audit and planning-state advancement

## Out of Scope

- OID4VP verifier flows beyond the credential-format milestone
- public wallet networks
- new verifier families outside the `v2.13` boundary
