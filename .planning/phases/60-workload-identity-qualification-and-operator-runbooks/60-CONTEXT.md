# Phase 60: Workload Identity Qualification and Operator Runbooks - Context

## Goal

Close `v2.12` with qualification evidence, replay and failure documentation,
and operator runbooks for workload identity and attestation verifier bridges.

## Why This Phase Exists

The milestone only counts if the new verifier and workload-identity surfaces
are operationally legible. This phase packages the proof and runbook boundary
for operators and reviewers.

## Scope

- qualification lanes for workload identity and verifier bridges
- release and partner-facing docs for supported boundary
- operator runbooks for verifier failure, replay, and recovery

## Out of Scope

- new verifier adapters or new identity formats beyond phases 57-59
