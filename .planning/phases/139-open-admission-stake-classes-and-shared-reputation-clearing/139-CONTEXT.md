# Phase 139: Open-Admission Stake Classes and Shared-Reputation Clearing - Context

## Goal

Define bounded open-admission classes plus shared reputation clearing with anti-sybil and anti-oracle protections.

## Why This Phase Exists

Open admission and shared reputation only make sense after the federation and replication contracts are explicit.

## Scope

- open-admission classes
- stake or bond participation models
- shared-reputation clearing
- anti-sybil and anti-oracle controls

## Out of Scope

- final federation qualification
- public wallet directory
- broader DID/VC method support
