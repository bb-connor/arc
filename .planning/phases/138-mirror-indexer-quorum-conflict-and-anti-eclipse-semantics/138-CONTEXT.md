# Phase 138: Mirror/Indexer Quorum, Conflict, and Anti-Eclipse Semantics - Context

## Goal

Define how mirrors and indexers participate in federation state with quorum, conflict resolution, and anti-eclipse protections.

## Why This Phase Exists

Shared trust state is not credible unless federation visibility is resilient against stale, conflicting, or eclipsed publishers.

## Scope

- quorum and conflict semantics
- anti-eclipse rules
- mirror and indexer federation roles
- freshness and conflict evidence

## Out of Scope

- open admission classes
- shared reputation clearing
- identity-network routing
