# Phase 129: Web3 Trust Boundary, Identity Binding, and Protocol Freeze - Context

## Goal

Freeze the web3 trust boundary, operator identity binding, proof bundle format, dispute posture, and multichain consistency rules.

## Why This Phase Exists

Web3 execution should not begin until ARC commits to its trust boundary and on-chain/off-chain authority semantics.

## Scope

- web3 trust-boundary decisions
- operator identity binding
- settlement evidence and dispute policy
- multichain consistency and recovery rules

## Out of Scope

- contract implementation
- anchoring daemon operation
- live settlement dispatch
