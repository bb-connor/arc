# Plan 111-01

Define allocation-decision artifacts for governed actions.

## Scope

- allocation decision shape and linkage to governed approvals
- explicit source-of-funds references and reserved amounts
- decision outcomes such as allocate, queue, manual review, and deny

## Success Criteria

- governed actions map to one explicit allocation decision
- source-of-funds selection is deterministic and auditable
- unsupported capital configurations fail closed
