# Phase 89: Provider Registry, Coverage Classes, and Jurisdiction Policy - Context

## Goal

Define a curated provider registry and policy model for coverage classes,
jurisdictions, currencies, and evidence requirements.

## Why This Phase Exists

Liability-market orchestration cannot start with quote requests. ARC first
needs one explicit provider and jurisdiction policy layer so later quote,
bind, and claim workflows remain bounded and auditable.

## Scope

- provider registry schema
- coverage classes and jurisdiction policy
- currency and evidence requirement declarations
- curated provider admission rules

## Out of Scope

- quote or bind workflow execution
- claim adjudication lifecycle
- permissionless provider federation
