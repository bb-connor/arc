# Phase 77: Certification Criteria and Conformance Evidence Profiles - Context

## Goal

Version ARC certification criteria and evidence packages so public discovery
can be based on reproducible conformance profiles rather than ad hoc operator
claims.

## Why This Phase Exists

The research treats certification as part of a broader market substrate. ARC
cannot widen discovery honestly until the evidence behind a certification is
stable, comparable, and versioned across operators.

## Scope

- versioned certification criteria
- reproducible evidence-package profiles
- conformance terminology and publisher provenance rules
- fail-closed handling for incomplete or unverifiable certification evidence

## Out of Scope

- public discovery network implementation
- dispute workflows and governance closure
- auto-trust semantics from certification presence
