# Phase 91: Claim Packages, Disputes, and Liability Adjudication - Context

## Goal

Add immutable claim packages, provider responses, dispute state, and
adjudication evidence linked back to receipts, exposure, and bond state.

## Why This Phase Exists

The research's liability-market endgame is not just quoting and placement. ARC
also needs one truthful representation of what was claimed, how a provider
responded, and how disputes were adjudicated over canonical evidence.

## Scope

- claim-package and provider-response artifacts
- dispute and adjudication lifecycle
- linkage to receipts, exposure, and bond events
- fail-closed evidence and jurisdiction checks

## Out of Scope

- final marketplace qualification and boundary rewrite
- public provider discovery beyond the curated registry
- insurer, lender, or broker-of-record behavior
