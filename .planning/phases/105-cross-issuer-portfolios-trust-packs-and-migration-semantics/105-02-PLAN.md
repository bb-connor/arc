# Plan 105-02

Define migration and composition semantics across issuers.

## Scope

- migration of identities, credentials, and appraisal artifacts across issuers
- collision handling for overlapping subject or issuer identifiers
- compatibility and attenuation rules for mixed-issuer evidence

## Success Criteria

- multi-issuer composition rules are explicit and auditable
- collisions and ambiguous provenance fail closed
- migration does not invent synthetic aggregate trust
