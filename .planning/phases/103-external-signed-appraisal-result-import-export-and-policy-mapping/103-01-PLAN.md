# Plan 103-01

Define the signed appraisal export and import artifacts.

## Scope

- signed appraisal result envelope and metadata
- issuer, verifier, and subject provenance fields
- replay-safe identifiers, timestamps, and signature requirements

## Success Criteria

- ARC can export one signed portable appraisal result
- imported results preserve provenance and integrity requirements
- signature and freshness failures are explicit and fail closed
