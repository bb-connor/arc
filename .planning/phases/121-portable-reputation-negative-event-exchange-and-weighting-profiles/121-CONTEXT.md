# Phase 121: Portable Reputation, Negative-Event Exchange, and Weighting Profiles - Context

## Goal

Externalize portable reputation and negative-event exchange while preserving
issuer provenance and local weighting rather than inventing a global trust
oracle.

## Why This Phase Exists

The open-market endgame needs portable market-discipline evidence, but ARC must
keep reputation exchange bounded, provenance-preserving, and locally weighted.

## Scope

- portable reputation and negative-event artifacts
- issuer provenance and attenuation semantics
- local weighting profiles and import policy boundaries
- fail-closed handling for unverifiable or contradictory reputation inputs

## Out of Scope

- fee schedules, bonds, and slashing economics
- adversarial multi-operator qualification
- final release-boundary closure
