# Summary 123-02

Proved visibility-versus-trust separation under adversarial multi-operator
conditions.

## Delivered

- invalid mirrored listing signatures remain visible to search and
  transparency consumers but are excluded from admitted results
- divergent replica freshness now produces explicit non-admission instead of
  silent trust widening
- imported portable reputation remains locally weighted and rejects disallowed
  or contradictory remote evidence fail closed
- governance and open-market penalty evaluation now reject trust activations
  that were not issued by the governing local operator

## Result

Hostile public data can stay observable without becoming runtime trust,
sanction authority, or market-admission truth.
