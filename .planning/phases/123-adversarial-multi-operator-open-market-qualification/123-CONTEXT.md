# Phase 123: Adversarial Multi-Operator Open-Market Qualification - Context

## Goal

Prove the open-market model under adversarial multi-operator conditions without
collapsing visibility, listing, or imported evidence into trust.

## Why This Phase Exists

The final endgame claim is not credible unless ARC can show adversarial
multi-operator behavior across registry, governance, reputation, and economic
controls.

## Scope

- adversarial multi-operator qualification matrix
- negative-path coverage across registry, reputation, governance, and economics
- partner-reviewable evidence for open-market behavior
- fail-closed treatment for stale, conflicting, or malicious operator data

## Out of Scope

- final release-audit and protocol closeout
- hosted public-launch observation
- new feature families beyond the defined endgame ladder
