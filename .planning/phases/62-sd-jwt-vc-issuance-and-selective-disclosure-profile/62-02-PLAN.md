# Plan 62-02

Define the selective-disclosure contract for ARC portable credentials.

## Scope

- choose which claims may be disclosed selectively
- define which claims are always required and must remain anchored
- bind disclosure behavior to explicit policy and verifier request shape

## Success Criteria

- over-disclosure is prevented by contract rather than convention
- verifier-required fields remain stable and anchored
- disclosure rules are explainable to operators and reviewers
