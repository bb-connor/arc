# Phase 84: Credit Qualification, Backtests, and Provider Risk Package - Context

## Goal

Close `v2.18` with backtests, simulation, and one provider-facing risk package
strong enough to justify external capital review.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC should not claim a credit-grade economic layer until exposure, score, and
facility outputs are replayable, explainable, and packaged honestly for
external reviewers.

## Scope

- historical backtests and simulation
- provider risk package and reviewer exports
- qualification over score and facility failure modes
- milestone audit and planning-state advancement into `v2.19`

## Out of Scope

- reserve-backed execution gating
- liability-market provider workflows
- broad historical cleanup
