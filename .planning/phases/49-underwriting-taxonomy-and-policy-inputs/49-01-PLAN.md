Define the underwriting taxonomy, evidence references, and typed policy-input
contract that later phases will evaluate and sign.

## Work

- add core underwriting types for risk class, decision reason, evidence

## Verification

- unit coverage for underwriting taxonomy serialization and validation
- regression coverage proving evidence references remain provenance-aware and
