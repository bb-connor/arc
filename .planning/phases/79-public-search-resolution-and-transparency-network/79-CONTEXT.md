# Phase 79: Public Search, Resolution, and Transparency Network - Context

## Goal

Add public search and transparency surfaces over certification artifacts while
keeping consumer admission policy evidence-backed and operator-controlled.

## Why This Phase Exists

The research points toward marketplace formation, but ARC's public discovery
must still preserve the core rule that listings do not auto-grant trust. This
phase adds search and transparency without collapsing those boundaries.

## Scope

- public search and comparison over certification listings
- transparency feeds or logs for listing state, updates, and provenance
- explicit consume and resolve boundaries for marketplace users
- fail-closed behavior for stale, superseded, or revoked listings

## Out of Scope

- disputes and governance closure
- economic market or liability placement logic
- automatic runtime trust from listing visibility
