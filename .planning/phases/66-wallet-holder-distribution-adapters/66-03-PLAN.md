# Plan 66-03

Define coexistence and fail-closed boundaries between OID4VP and ARC-native
holder transport.

## Scope

- document when operators should use OID4VP versus ARC-native challenge flows
- keep policy, lifecycle, and verifier semantics aligned across both lanes
- add regression coverage for unsupported wallet or adapter behavior

## Success Criteria

- the dual-lane portability story is explicit instead of ambiguous
- ARC-native challenge flows remain supported without blocking OID4VP
- boundary and downgrade failures are documented and tested
