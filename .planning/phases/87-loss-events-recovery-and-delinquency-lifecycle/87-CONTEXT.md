# Phase 87: Loss Events, Recovery, and Delinquency Lifecycle - Context

## Goal

Add immutable loss, recovery, delinquency, reserve-release, and write-off state
over bond-backed execution.

## Why This Phase Exists

Bonded autonomy is incomplete until ARC can record what happens when economic
backing is consumed, impaired, restored, or written off. The research's market
discipline thesis depends on explicit loss and recovery lifecycle truth.

## Scope

- loss-event and delinquency artifacts
- recovery, reserve-release, and write-off lifecycle
- linkage back to exposure and bond state
- fail-closed settlement or reserve adjustments

## Out of Scope

- end-to-end qualification and sandbox proof
- provider quote and claim flows
- broad cleanup or release archival
