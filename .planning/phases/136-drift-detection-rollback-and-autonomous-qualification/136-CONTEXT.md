# Phase 136: Drift Detection, Rollback, and Autonomous Qualification - Context

## Goal

Prove autonomous insurance automation can detect drift, fail safe, explain itself, and roll back without hidden widening.

## Why This Phase Exists

The pricing milestone only closes once ARC can qualify autonomous behavior as a bounded, reversible, operator-reviewable lane.

## Scope

- drift detection and rollback controls
- explanation and comparison reports
- fail-safe automation posture
- milestone qualification and boundary closure

## Out of Scope

- cross-operator federation
- public wallet routing
- new execution rails beyond the official web3 stack
