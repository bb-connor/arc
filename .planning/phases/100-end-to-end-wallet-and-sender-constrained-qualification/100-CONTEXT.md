# Phase 100: End-to-End Wallet and Sender-Constrained Qualification - Context

## Goal

Close `v2.22` with qualification evidence over wallet exchange, optional
identity assertion, and sender-constrained runtime behavior.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC should not claim wallet and sender-constrained interop until same-device,
cross-device, async exchange, and negative sender-proof paths are all
qualified and documented honestly.

## Scope

- same-device and cross-device wallet qualification
- asynchronous or message-oriented exchange proof
- sender-constrained negative-path coverage
- release and partner-boundary updates

## Out of Scope

- wider wallet ecosystem claims beyond the bounded supported matrix
- next-milestone verifier federation or appraisal interop work
- hosted workflow observation for external release publication
