# Plan 102-02

Define the reason taxonomy used by appraisal decisions and imports.

## Scope

- standardized reason codes for pass, warn, deny, degrade, and unknown states
- reason grouping for verifier trust, evidence freshness, measurement mismatch, and debug posture
- replay-safe and audit-friendly reason semantics

## Success Criteria

- reason codes are consistent across verifier families
- policy and operator surfaces can explain decisions with one shared taxonomy
- ambiguous reasons fail closed instead of being normalized silently
