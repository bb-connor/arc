# Phase 113: Executable Reserve Impairment, Release, and Slash Controls - Context

## Goal

Make reserve impairment, release, and slash controls executable under explicit
evidence, authority, and appeal rules.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC's endgame includes bonded autonomy and market discipline. That requires
reserve controls to move from descriptive posture into explicit executable
artifacts.

## Scope

- executable reserve impairment, release, and slash controls
- evidence-linked authority and appeal semantics
- reconciliation-aware reserve state transitions
- fail-closed handling for stale authority or contradictory evidence

## Out of Scope

- delegated pricing or automatic coverage binding
- automatic claims payment and payout rails
- open registry and governance-network phases
