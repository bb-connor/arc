# Plan 113-02

Define authority, appeal, and reconciliation rules for reserve controls.

## Scope

- authority chains for impairment, release, and slash actions
- appeal windows and supersession behavior
- reconciliation mismatch, stale authority, and duplicate-action handling

## Success Criteria

- reserve controls require explicit valid authority
- appeal and reconciliation state are machine-readable
- stale or contradictory reserve actions fail closed
