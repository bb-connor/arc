# Plan 57-01

Define the workload-identity mapping contract.

## Scope

- introduce typed SPIFFE/SVID-style identity inputs and normalized ARC mapping
  outputs
- define validation and failure rules for malformed or ambiguous identifiers
- document which identity facts become policy-visible and which remain opaque

## Success Criteria

- ARC has one explicit typed workload-identity mapping contract
- unsupported or malformed workload identifiers fail closed
- the contract does not silently widen rights or trust
