# Plan 119-02

Define open admission classes and bounded eligibility semantics.

## Scope

- admission classes for public-but-untrusted, reviewable, bond-backed, or role-gated artifacts
- policy-visible eligibility criteria
- stale, missing, or incompatible admission handling

## Success Criteria

- admission classes are explicit and machine-readable
- eligibility rules do not hide local discretion or review
- incompatible artifacts fail closed
