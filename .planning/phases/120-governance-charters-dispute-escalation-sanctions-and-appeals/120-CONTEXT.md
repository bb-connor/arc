# Phase 120: Governance Charters, Dispute Escalation, Sanctions, and Appeals - Context

## Goal

Define portable governance charters, dispute-escalation, sanctions, freezes,
and appeal artifacts for the open registry network.

## Why This Phase Exists

Open publication and admission classes are not enough. The full endgame also
needs a portable governance layer for sanctions, disputes, and appeals across
operators.

## Scope

- governance charter artifacts
- dispute, sanction, freeze, and appeal lifecycle artifacts
- cross-operator escalation and evidence-handling rules
- milestone audit and public-boundary updates

## Out of Scope

- portable reputation weighting
- fee schedules and market-bond economics
- adversarial open-market qualification closure
