# Plan 120-02

Define escalation, sanction, and appeal lifecycle semantics.

## Scope

- escalation paths and counterparties
- sanction, freeze, supersession, and appeal transitions
- fail-closed handling for stale, conflicting, or unauthorized governance actions

## Success Criteria

- dispute escalation and appeal flow are explicit and reproducible
- governance actions require valid authority and scope
- conflicting or stale actions fail closed
