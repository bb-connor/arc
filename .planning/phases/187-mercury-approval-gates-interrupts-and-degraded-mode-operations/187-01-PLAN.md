# Plan 187-01

Encode explicit supervised-live approval, interruption, and rollback control
semantics for the same workflow.

## Scope

- extend the supervised-live capture contract with typed release and rollback
  gates, evidence-health state, coverage state, and interruption records
- expose the control-state summary through the successful supervised-live export
  output
- keep the safety contract Mercury-specific and layered on top of the existing
  ARC evidence-export path

## Success Criteria

- supervised-live captures declare approval and interruption posture explicitly
- successful exports surface auditable control-state summary data
- degraded or incomplete control state is distinguishable from export-ready
  supervised-live evidence
