# Plan 112-01

Build qualification evidence for live capital-book and allocation flows.

## Scope

- qualification for ledger, instruction, and allocation behavior
- negative-path coverage for mixed currency, stale authority, and missing counterparties
- reproducible commands and expected outcomes

## Success Criteria

- qualification proves the live capital surfaces end to end
- critical failure paths are regression-covered
- evidence is reproducible for operators and reviewers
