# Phase 112: Capital Execution Qualification and Regulated-Role Baseline - Context

## Goal

Qualify the live capital execution surface and formalize the regulated-role
baseline ARC assumes when money is being locked, allocated, or moved.

## Why This Phase Exists

ARC should not claim live capital execution until the role topology, failure
paths, and qualification matrix are explicit and reproducible.

## Scope

- live-capital qualification evidence
- regulated-role baseline and authority assumptions
- release and partner-boundary updates
- milestone audit and closeout

## Out of Scope

- executable reserve slashing or automatic claims payout
- open registry and governance-network work
- adversarial open-market qualification
