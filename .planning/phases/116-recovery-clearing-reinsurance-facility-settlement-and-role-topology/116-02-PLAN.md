# Plan 116-02

Define clearing and reconciliation semantics across counterparties.

## Scope

- intended versus reconciled recovery and settlement state
- mismatch, staleness, and counterparty-disagreement handling
- negative-path rules for missing authority or unresolved obligations

## Success Criteria

- counterparties can reconcile settlement state explicitly
- mismatch and disagreement paths are visible and fail closed
- clearing behavior is role-attributed and deterministic
