# Phase 116: Recovery Clearing, Reinsurance/Facility Settlement, and Role Topology - Context

## Goal

Close the live-money lifecycle across recoveries, reinsurance or facility
settlement, and counterparty role topology.

## Why This Phase Exists

The reserve and payout ladder is incomplete until ARC can represent how money
flows back across facilities, reinsurers, or recovery parties with explicit
roles and reconciliation truth.

## Scope

- recovery, reinsurance, and facility-settlement artifacts
- counterparty and role topology for live-money flows
- reconciliation and failure semantics across counterparties
- milestone qualification and closeout

## Out of Scope

- open registry publication and governance-network work
- portable reputation and marketplace-fee economics
- final endgame qualification closure
