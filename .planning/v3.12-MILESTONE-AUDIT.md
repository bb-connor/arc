# v3.12 Milestone Audit

## Milestone

`v3.12 Cross-Protocol Integrity and Truth Completion`

## Outcome

`v3.12` is complete locally on `2026-04-14`. The milestone goal is met: Chio's
shipped kernel/substrate breakthrough now has the missing credibility-closeout
work behind it. ACP live-path enforcement is cryptographically enforced, the
outward A2A/ACP edges expose truthful kernel-mediated execution helpers and
receipt behavior, the remaining weaker operator/runtime surfaces reached the
same production bar as the HTTP substrate, and the repo narrative now makes a
defensible distinction between today's shipped claim and the larger future
cross-protocol thesis.
