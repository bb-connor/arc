## Status

Milestone `v2.53` meets its implementation definition of done. Phases `221`
through `224` are implemented, verified, and keep the intended product
boundary intact: Mercury now packages one bounded release-readiness lane on
its own app surface without reintroducing Mercury logic into ARC generic
crates.

The resulting posture is specific:

- Mercury ships one `release-readiness` command family in `arc-mercury`
- `arc-mercury-core` defines one release-readiness profile and package
  contract
- the package stays rooted in proof, inquiry, assurance-suite, and trust-
  network artifacts that already exist on Mercury's own surface
- Mercury owns one operator release checklist, escalation manifest, and
  support handoff
- the milestone closes with one explicit
  `launch_release_readiness_only` decision rather than a generic ARC launch
  console claim or new product-line claim
