# Summary 314-03

Phase `314` packages the normative work from phases `311`-`313` into
standards-facing artifacts.

- `spec/ietf/draft-arc-protocol-00.md` captures ARC in an IETF-style
  standards-track document shape, including the surface model, versioning,
  errors, security considerations, and conformance lane.
- `docs/standards/ARC_PROTOCOL_ALIGNMENT_MATRIX.md` maps ARC concepts to GNAP,
  SCITT, RATS, RFC 9449, W3C VC, OID4VCI/VP, and RFC 8785 while explicitly
  recording the current boundary or gap for each mapping.

That gives the repo one bounded standards narrative tied directly to checked-in
specs and executable conformance evidence rather than to broad product claims.
