# Milestone Audit: v2.51 MERCURY Extensions Shared Service Boundaries, Cross-Product Governance, and Platform Hardening

## Status

Milestone `v2.51` meets its implementation definition of done. Phases `213`
through `216` are implemented, verified, and close one bounded post-Chio-Wall
hardening lane over the current MERCURY plus Chio-Wall product set without
collapsing product boundaries, widening Chio's generic substrate, or reopening
new buyer or connector programs.

The resulting posture is specific:

- Chio now exposes one generic `arc product-surface export` and `validate`
  boundary over the current product set
- the shared-service catalog is machine-readable and rooted in Chio-owned
  receipt, checkpoint, evidence-export, and verifier truth
- MERCURY and Chio-Wall remain separate product manifests with separate owned
  surfaces and non-goals
- cross-product governance is explicit for release, incident, and trust-
  material custody without implying a merged shell
- the hardening backlog is bounded to platform seams for the current product
  set and ends in one explicit `proceed_platform_hardening_only` decision

  explicit, the hardening backlog could sequence real platform work instead
