## Milestone

`v2.81 Deployable Chio and Developer Onboarding`

## Outcome

`v2.81` is complete locally on `2026-04-13`. The milestone goal is met: Chio is
now consistently branded in the shipped onboarding surface, `arc init`
scaffolds a governed hello-world project, both SDKs are qualified as stable
public packages, the Docker quickstart boots a hosted edge plus receipt viewer,
and the new progressive tutorial connects that deployable path to the official
framework examples.
