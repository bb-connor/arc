# Milestone Audit: v2.54 MERCURY Controlled Adoption, Renewal Evidence, and Reference Readiness

## Status

Milestone `v2.54` meets its implementation definition of done. Phases `225`
through `228` are implemented, verified, and keep the intended product
boundary intact: Mercury now packages one bounded controlled-adoption lane on
its own app surface without reintroducing Mercury logic into ARC generic
crates.

The resulting posture is specific:

- Mercury ships one `controlled-adoption` command family in `arc-mercury`
- `arc-mercury-core` defines one controlled-adoption profile and package
  contract
- the package stays rooted in release-readiness, trust-network,
  assurance-suite, proof, and inquiry artifacts that already exist on
  Mercury's own surface
- Mercury owns one customer-success checklist, renewal acknowledgement,
  reference-readiness brief, and support-escalation manifest

  `scale_controlled_adoption_only` decision rather than a generic ARC renewal
  console claim or new Mercury surface-family claim
